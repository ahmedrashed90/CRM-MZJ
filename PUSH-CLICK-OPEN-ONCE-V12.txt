MZJ CRM - Notification conversation open-once fix v12

- Notification click still opens the app and the related customer conversation.
- The pending customer request is consumed immediately after the conversation opens.
- Closing the conversation clears all pending notification-open keys.
- Refreshing or reopening the app no longer opens the previous customer again.
- Old v10/v11 requests without a timestamp are removed automatically.
- A notification-open request expires automatically after 15 minutes if it cannot be matched.
- No page, permission, distribution, report, or dashboard design logic was changed.
